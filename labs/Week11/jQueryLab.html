Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 and a 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 and a 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Place softened butter in a bowl and mix together sugars until smooth.
  2. Mix in egg and vanilla extract until combined.
  3. Mix in baking soda, salt, and slowly incorporate flour.
  4. Slowly mix in chocolate chips.
  5. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper, drop 2 TBS of dough.
  6. Chill for at least 2 hours.
  7. Preheat oven to 350.
  8. Space cookies 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 11-15 minutes, or until the edges are a light golden and the tops are no longer glossy.
  10. Let cool on the cookie sheets for at least 10 minutes before removing.
